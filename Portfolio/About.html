My design philosophy

"If we do not listen, we can only use "our ways" to help others blend into "our culture"." I found myself a flexible person who embraces differences, understand individual needs and is capable of taking on challenges to solve problems.

UX and me

From the interviews with new immigrants in Taiwan for a research project, I found that many of them were struggling to find information or not comfortable to make the most of digital platform to solve their problem. Foreign workers and domestic helpers need more information related to their work environment as well as marriage and psychological counseling since they mostly live far from home and may be experiencing issues related to their family. However, these immigrants are always presented with Taiwanese and culture learning courses from the outside community, which are not the most helpful resource for them. This experience made me realize the value of UX professionals, as we can act as a bridge to connect information and human, empathize with users and see the world through their eyes.
