Introduction

Story

In keeping with the NDA, I cannot show you all of our products, but I am happy to talk about the story!

Before I came to Michigan to pursue my master's degree, I was a member of UI/UX design team at Deila Inc., Taiwan, which is a small size, early-stage startup focusing on the area of travel. We launched an innovative idea of providing a new, user-centered online platform of intermediary between unfamiliar travelers, local tour guides and transportation industry. I was taking on the responsibilities of optimizing user experiences by designing website interface (I was the only person!), interviewing users and conducting usability testing. Particularly, I also worked on most of the content of the website, so if you find some interesting ideas, it is highly likely that they are my output!
